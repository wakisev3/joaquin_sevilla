Bio

Welcome! I am Joaquin Sevilla, a graduate student of economics at De La Salle University in Manila. I am also a research assistant at the Angelo King Institute, and a teaching assistant for microeconomic theory and economic development courses. My research interests are in international trade and tourism, firm dynamics, and econometrics. I am currently doing research on firm dynamics in the Philippines. You can view my CV here.
