Welcome!

I am Joaquin Sevilla, a graduate student, research assistant, and teaching assistant at the Department of Economics, De La Salle University – Manila. I am also a research assistant at the University of Oslo, under the ACCESS ERC Consolidator Grant. My research interests span international trade, economic geography, and the role of firms in economic development.

Most of my current work uses longitudinal plant- and product-level data to examine the role of trade, trade and industrial policies, and globalization more broadly in determining firm outcomes in a developing country context. See my research →
