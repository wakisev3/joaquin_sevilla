About — Bio

Welcome! I am Joaquin Sevilla, a graduate student and research assistant at the Department of Economics of De La Salle University in Manila. My research interests span international trade, development, and applied econometrics. Most of my work involve large longitudinal firm- and product-level datasets, where I ask how trade and trade policy affect firm dynamics.
