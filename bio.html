About — Bio

Welcome! I am Joaquin Sevilla, a graduate student of economics at De La Salle University in Manila. I am also a research assistant at the Angelo King Institute, and a teaching assistant. My research interests include development economics, firm dynamics, and macroeconomic modelling for emerging economies.

Roles

  • Research Assistant — Angelo King Institute (AKI)
  • Teaching Assistant — Undergraduate Economics

Research Interests

Firm growth and productivity, trade and industrial policy, macroeconomic modelling (DSGE), impact evaluation methods, and applied microeconometrics.
